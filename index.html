More About Me

I volunteered with the Mujeres de Frente collective in Ecuador, where we gave robotics lessons during their summer school. Using the Tinkering Labs Kit, we built playful machines with the children of mothers who are part of the educational program. The activities let everyone explore, test ideas, and work together. It was a fun experience that I hope to be part of again next year.
